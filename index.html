The Serverless Web.

Fabric, the simple protocol for distributed applications.

Built on Bitcoin

Fabric is an extension to the Bitcoin protocol, allowing applications to built and deployed today. Changes to Bitcoin are not necessary.

Proof of Service

Nodes on the Fabric network are rewarded for their efforts, proportional to their contributions, using self-enforcing contracts.

The Team

We're a team of dreamers and builders, and we have a vision for what the web could have been. We all share in our passion for a truly decentralized web, and plan to use our experiences from our former lives to realize a server-free, censorship-proof future.
